NDC Porto is a leading developer conference held annually in Porto, Portugal. It brings together speakers and attendees from across the software ecosystem to share practical talks, workshops, and deep dives on .NET, web, cloud, and modern development practices. The event offers a friendly, community-driven atmosphere for learning and networking. Developers of all levels attend to discover new techniques, collaborate with peers, and stay current with industry trends and tooling.

Each year the program features a balanced mix of keynote talks, breakout sessions, and hands-on workshops. Speakers are chosen for both depth of expertise and the ability to make complex topics approachable — from cutting-edge language features to pragmatic architecture advice. Workshops provide extended, instructor-led time to dig into topics like performance tuning, testing strategies, cloud-native design, and developer tooling. Attendees often leave with runnable code samples and a clearer roadmap for adopting new practices back at work.

The conference emphasizes community and mentorship: lightning talks, birds-of-a-feather meetups, and dedicated newcomer tracks make it easy to connect with peers, find collaborators, and get practical feedback on ideas or challenges you’re facing.
